Welcome To My Final Project

In my final project game I made a game called Snake Pac-Man, the object of the game is to get 15 lemons without dying by the ghosts. The ghost can either take on of your lives or it can kill you on impact.
There is a timer that keeps track of how long it took you to get a score of 15 within the lemons.
You play by using the mousepad to move yourself and you click on the lemons to be able to get a score.
